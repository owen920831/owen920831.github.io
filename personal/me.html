About me

您好，我的名字叫做潘茗脩，我現在就讀於清華大學資工系二年級。我最近的興趣是調酒與品飲各種風味的雞尾酒與烈酒。

Hi, my name is Pan Ming-Xiu, I am currently studying in the second year of Tsinghua University's Department of Computer Science. Recently, I am interesting in bartending and variety of flavors and cocktails.

在所有烈酒之中，琴酒是我的最愛，根據蒸餾的方式與原物料的不同，他的風味非常豐富及多變。

Of all the spirits, Gin is my favorite, for it has a very rich and varied flavor depending on the way the wine is made and the ingredients.

在品飲烈酒與雞尾酒時，香氣占鑑賞中的很大一部分。

When drinking spirits and cocktails, aroma takes a large part of appreciation.

琴酒原酒通常有柑橘、木本、草本等等調性，在蒸餾的過程中，我們可以加入胡椒、馬告等等辛香料讓風味變化。

Gin original wine usually has citrus, woody, herbal and other tonalities. During the distillation process, we can add pepper, maltol and other spices to change the flavor.
